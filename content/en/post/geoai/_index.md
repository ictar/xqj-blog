> GeoAI = Geographic Information Science (GIS) + Artificial Intelligence (AI)

This series aims to systematically introduce the **basic concepts, methods, and applications of GeoAI**, ranging from spatial data representation, machine learning and deep learning methods, to large models and practical cases. The content is designed to help you go from basic understanding to hands-on practice.
